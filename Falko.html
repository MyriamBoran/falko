Falko Konditormeister

Who is Falko?

Falko is from Heilbronn, near Stuttgart in Germany. He is a Konditormeister, a state qualified Master Pastry Chef, who moved to Edinburgh in 1998 because he loved the city but found that the types of cakes in most places were somewhat different to his German and Austrian baking style. He is all about restrained amounts of sugar and subtle, natural flavours.

"A cake should have balanced textures and flavours; it shouldn't be overly decorated and overly sweet to make up for the blandness inside."

You can visit us at either of our two Konditorei shops in Edinburgh or Gullane, East Lothian. We also attend Edinburgh Farmers' Market each week and Balerno Farmers' Market on the second Saturday of each month. See you there?
